Coding Clubs

DECODERS

DeCoders is a student club in Siddaganga Institute of Technology, Tumakuru, founded in the year 2014 by a group of tech enthusiasts. We are a team of tech-savvy students who are driven by the urge to get a knack of various technological aspects. DeCoders aims at helping its members and the students of SIT in enhancing their problem solving, programming and development-based skills as well as intrapersonal skills by conducting various competitions, sessions and tech-talks throughout the year.

SARK

SARK is a proactive technical group based out of SIT, Tumakuru, dedicated to organizing year-round technical events for students. It emphasizes project-based learning to stay aligned with the ever-evolving industry demands. The group prioritizes giving back to society while aiming to digitalize the entire social structure. SARK's initiatives foster a hands-on learning environment, preparing students to meet contemporary technological challenges. This commitment ensures that members are well-equipped with the skills needed to drive societal and digital transformation.

GEEKS FOR GEEKS

Geeks for Geeks (GFG) is a dynamic technical group rooted in SIT, Tumakuru, thriving on the principle of peer learning. It is a collaborative space where passionate Geeks come together to innovate and develop solutions for real-world challenges. GFG fosters a vibrant community, encouraging knowledge sharing and mutual growth among its members. By leveraging collective expertise, the group aims to tackle complex problems and drive technological advancement. This unique environment nurtures creativity, teamwork, and practical problem-solving skills
